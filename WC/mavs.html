Dallas Mavericks

The 2014–15 Dallas Mavericks season was the 35th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA). The Mavericks finished fourth in the Southwest division and seventh in the Western conference with a 50–32 record. The season ended with a 1-4 first round playoff series loss to the Houston Rockets.

New Orleans Pelicans

The 2014–15 New Orleans Pelicans season is the 13th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA) and 2nd under the "Pelicans" name.[1] Despite finishing last in the Southwest division for the fourth straight season, the Pelicans finished with a 45-37 record and clinched the eighth seed in the Western conference. The Pelicans clinch the playoffs for the first time since 2011 when the team name was the Hornets. The season ended at 0-4 first round playoff series to the Golden State Warriors.

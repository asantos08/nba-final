Houston Rockets

The 2014–15 Houston Rockets season is the 48th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA), and the 44th in the Houston area.[1] The Rockets finished the regular season with a 56–26 record, the third best in franchise history. They also won their first ever Southwest Division title and first Division crown since 1994. The Rockets beat the Dallas Mavericks in the first round of the playoffs, advancing to the Western Conference Semifinals for the first time since 2009.

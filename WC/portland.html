The 2014–15 Portland Trail Blazers season is the 45th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA).[1] The Trail Blazers finished the regular-season record at 51-31, but they were able to capture the franchise's first Northwest division title. They were eliminated by Memphis Grizzlies in the first round (4-1).

Indiana Pacers

The 2014–15 Indiana Pacers season is the 47th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA).[1] The Pacers finished fourth in Central division and ninth in Eastern conference. The Pacers missed the playoffs for the first time since 2010 after a 56-26 mark from the previous season, following a two-straight Eastern Conference Finals appearances.

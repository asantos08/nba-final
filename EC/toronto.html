Toronto Raptors

The 2014–15 Toronto Raptors season is the 20th season of the franchise in the National Basketball Association (NBA).[1] The Raptors repeat their Atlantic division title for the first time in franchise history, finishing fourth place in Eastern conference, won 49 games during the regular season, breaking their franchise record of 48 wins in the previous season. Despite the achievement, the fourth-seeded Raptors were unexpectedly swept in the opening round of the playoffs by the fifth-seeded Washington Wizards as they continue to extend their post-season series victory drought. The defeat was primarily blamed on the team's poor defense which was notably exploited by the Wizards.
